Welcome

“Start children off on the way they should go, and even when they are old they will not turn from it.” – Proverbs 22:6 (NIV)

At Foundations at Home Education Consulting, I help families design learning environments that support the whole child – academically, emotionally, and spiritually. Whether you're just starting homeschooling or navigating learning challenges, you don't have to walk that journey alone.

About Jenny

I'm Jenny Henderson, a retired special education teacher and reading coach with a heart for children and families. After decades in the classroom, I now partner with homeschool parents who want structure, peace, and confidence as they teach at home.

I specialize in working with diverse learners, including children with ADHD, autism, dyslexia, and other learning differences, as well as students who simply need a different approach than traditional school.

My goal is to create a safe, encouraging space where parents feel supported and children are seen for their strengths.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you work with?

Primarily preschool through middle school, with flexibility depending on your child’s needs.

Do you work with children with disabilities?

Yes. My background is in special education, and I have extensive experience with diverse learners.

Do you only work with Christian families?

No. I warmly welcome all families. Faith-based guidance is offered for those who request it.

Can you help if we’re already homeschooling but it feels chaotic?

Absolutely. Many families come to me when things feel overwhelming, and together we create a calmer, more sustainable plan.
