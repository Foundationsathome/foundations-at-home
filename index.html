Support for Your Homeschool Journey

Homeschool planning, reading support, and special education guidance — all from home.

At Foundations at Home Education Consulting, I help families build confident, calm, and effective homeschool routines. With over 30 years of experience as a special education teacher and reading coach, I partner with you to create a plan that fits your child.

Book a Free Inquiry Call View Services & Pricing

Is this you?

  • • New to homeschooling and not sure where to start?
  • • Worried your child is “behind” in reading or math?
  • • Parenting a child with ADHD, dyslexia, or autism?
  • • Feeling overwhelmed by curriculum choices and schedules?

You are not alone. Together, we’ll build strong foundations for learning — right at home.

Meet Your Homeschool Consultant

Warm, experienced guidance for families who want structure, confidence, and support in their homeschool.

Founder • Jenny Henderson

I’m a retired special education teacher and reading coach with more than three decades of experience supporting children of all abilities.

I understand the joys and challenges of helping children learn — and I know how overwhelming homeschooling can feel, especially when you’re navigating learning differences, reading struggles, or multiple grade levels at once.

Through Foundations at Home Education Consulting, I partner with parents to create personalized homeschool plans, reading supports, and practical strategies that work in real homes with real kids.

I believe every child can learn with the right tools and every parent can teach with the right support. I’d be honored to walk alongside your family.

What I Specialize In

  • ✔ Homeschool planning & daily schedules
  • ✔ Reading & literacy support
  • ✔ Support for ADHD, autism, dyslexia & learning differences
  • ✔ Gentle structure for overwhelmed parents
  • ✔ IEP-style individualized learning plans (for homeschool)

2

4
