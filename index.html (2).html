About Jenny

Retired Special Education Teacher • Reading Coach • Homeschool Consultant

I'm Jenny Henderson, a retired special education teacher and reading coach with a heart for children and families. After decades in the classroom, I now partner with homeschool parents who want structure, peace, and confidence as they teach at home.

I specialize in working with diverse learners, including children with ADHD, autism, dyslexia, and other learning differences, as well as students who simply need a gentler, more individualized approach than traditional school.

My approach is warm, practical, and faith-informed for families who desire it. Every child can learn, and every parent can grow more confident with the right tools and support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you work with?

Primarily preschool through middle school, with flexibility depending on your child’s needs.

Do you work with children with disabilities?

Yes. My background is in special education, and I have extensive experience with diverse learning needs.

Do you only work with Christian families?

No. I warmly welcome all families. Faith-based guidance is offered for those who request it.

Can you help if we’re already homeschooling but it feels chaotic?

Absolutely. Many families come to me when things feel overwhelming. Together, we create a calmer, more sustainable plan.

How do sessions take place?

Most sessions are held online via Zoom, with phone and limited in-person options depending on location.
